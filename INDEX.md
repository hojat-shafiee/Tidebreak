## üé® Game Features Overview

### Items to Collect
- ‚≠ê **Star** (Gold) - 100 points
- üíé **Diamond** (Green) - 75 points
- ‚ù§Ô∏è **Heart** (Pink) - 50 points
- üî∫ **Spike** (Red) - -30 points
- ‚ò†Ô∏è **Poison** (Purple) - -50 points

### Scoring System
- Base points per item
- Combo multiplier (1x, 2x, 3x, ...)
- Combo resets on negative items or misses
- Minimum score of 0

### Difficulty
- Difficulty increases every 15 seconds
- Spawn rate increases by 20% per wave
- Item speed increases by 15% per wave
- Game over when you miss 5 items

### Audio
- Unique sounds for each item type
- Wave progression chime
- Miss and game over sounds
- Fully dynamic (Web Audio API)
